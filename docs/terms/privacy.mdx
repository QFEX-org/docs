## Introduction

We at QFEX Bermuda Ltd. ("QFEX," "we," "us," or "our") respect and protect the privacy of those who explore our waitlist and referral program (the "Program") and visit our website ("Users") and Users who sign up for and access our Program ("Participants") (together referred throughout this policy as "you" and "your").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you explore, sign up for, or access our Program, which includes the services offered on our website and related services.

By accessing and using our Program, you accept this Privacy Policy and its terms. If you do not wish for your personal information to be collected, used, or disclosed as described in this Privacy Policy, or you are under 18 years of age, you should stop accessing our Program.

QFEX is subject to Bermuda law and regulation, including the Investment Business Act 2003 and the Personal Information Protection Act 2016\.

**Key Highlights:**

- We collect and use your information to provide and improve our Program, protect security, and meet legal obligations  
- We share your information with trusted third parties and service providers to offer our Program and fulfill legal requirements  
- You have privacy rights to request access to or deletion of information we hold about you  
- If you have questions, please contact us at [privacy@qfex.com](mailto:privacy@qfex.com)

We retain your information as needed to provide our Program, comply with legal obligations, or protect our interests. Retention periods vary based on:

## 7\. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain privacy rights. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at [privacy@qfex.com](mailto:privacy@qfex.com) or through your account settings.

### Available Rights May Include:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information  
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete information  
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information  
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your information  
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests  
- **Portability:** Receive your information in a portable format  
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for consent-based processing

### Informational Communications

You can opt out of informational communications through:

- Account settings  
- Unsubscribe links in emails  
- Contacting our support team
